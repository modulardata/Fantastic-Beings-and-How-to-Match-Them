Description

It's time to finish the mechanics of our game: when the creatures are matched, new animals should show up in the vacant cells.

After this, you're past the hardest part. All that's left is adding some finishing touches to our game. But more on that in the next stages :)

Objectives

  • Firstly, you need to implement the new window.generateRandomBeingName() method. This method is used to fill the empty cells that remain after matching and removing sequences. The result of this method is a string containing the name of one of the creature types. The method must randomly select and return one of the strings from the ['zouwu', 'swooping', 'salamander', 'puffskein', 'kelpie'] array.

  • Once the method is ready, you can build the mechanism for populating empty cells with random creatures - new creatures show up in empty cells (the names of these creatures are produced by the window.generateRandomBeingName() method). Further detail on the necessary mechanics can be found in the examples. If, after filling the empty cells, new sequences of identical creatures form, they also need to be removed.
